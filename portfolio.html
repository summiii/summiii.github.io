About Me

I'm Amal Sara — a Data Science student and AI engineer with a double life as a digital artist and graphic designer. I've been building things with code and color since 2020.

My technical work lives in NLP, machine learning, and building smart systems. My creative work lives in abstract art, social media design, and brand visuals. Most of my best projects happen where those two worlds collide.

I speak three languages fluently (Arabic, French, English), which means I can write, proofread, and translate across cultures — not just words, but tone and intent.

I work fast, communicate clearly, and actually care about the quality of what I produce. I'm based in Morocco and take clients worldwide.
